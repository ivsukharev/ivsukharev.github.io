Outsourced management.

Major business growth in this region typically requires local control and day-to-day driving of the business. Red’s outsourced management model provides a dynamic and efficient alternative to running your own operation.

We also provide project management support. In Russia’s high-grothw environment, companies are frequently faced with shortage of resources relative to the oppurtunities identified. Red offers dedicated project management to bring focus to a key initiative.

Key business benefits include:

Lower cost throught shared office & support infrastructure

More rapid route to market

Stronger focus on business development - not admin

Flexibility to expand or reduce resources as needed
